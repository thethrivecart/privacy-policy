Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Email Cleanup Assistant Chrome Extension handles your data. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we use your information.

Information We Access

The extension requires access to your Gmail account via Google OAuth for the following purposes:

  • Listing promotional and specific sender emails
  • Allowing you to delete selected emails
  • Allowing you to block emails from specific senders

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your Gmail content or personal information to any external server. All processing is done locally within the extension using your browser's environment.
